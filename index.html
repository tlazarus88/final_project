Each year the fine people at Brokelyn.com curate and offer a Beer Book, containing tickets for 1 free beer at an array of local pubs, bars, and breweries across Brooklyn. This website will help the holders of Beer Book Vol. 12: Middle Brooklyn Edition decide which bar would best suit their current imbibing needs before the tickets expire December 31, 2016.
